Achievements

CHAPTER 01

>

Deathshead’s Compound

In this chapter, you are introduced to the main character and his supporting character while flying a bomber assaulting a Nazi compound. You play Captain William "B.J" Blazkowicz as a special forces soldier assaulting Deathshead’s compound. The two supporting characters are Fergus and Wyatt, Fergus is flying the bomber as a clearly experienced vet to war while Wyatt is a young wet-behind-the-ears soldier who is constantly on the verge of losing their mind from the war around them. You immediately see how outnumbered the allied forces are, but Blazko and his team push on and make it to the inner compound. This is where Blazko needs to make a very difficult decision. The story splits into two timelines at this point. You can play the Fergus timeline or the Wyatt timeline.

Spoiler: At the end of the chapter, Deathshead has you choose either Fergus to kill or Wyatt to kill. You find out later they are not dead but extracted from their bodies and put into a war machine.

<

CHAPTER 02

>

Asylum

In this chapter, you are introduced to the character Anya, who is a nurse at her families health care practice. There is a short cinematic where it is shown that Anya and her family take care of you. As time goes by, you experience it all in a vegetative state. After some time, a Nazi strike team under Deathshead’s control is ordered to eliminate all disabled people at Anya’s family practice. BJ can hardly believe what he is seeing in his vegetative state and snaps out of it allowing you to play out the revenge he takes on the Nazi strike team, fighting against reinforcements and saving Anya.

Spoiler: You save Anya outside and all traces of anyone else are gone or dead. You hijack a car and start driving to Anya’s grandparent’s house with a Nazi officer as your hostage.

<

CHAPTER 03

>

A New World

In this chapter, BJ finds out from Anya’s grandparents that the allied forces lost to Germany. That there is no more war going on as the Nazi forces occupy the entire planet. During the escape and rescue of Anya from the hospital, they captured a Nazi officer. BJ goes on to interrogate the officer with a chainsaw. BJ finds out from the Nazi officer that there is still a resistance force and that they are kept in Eisenwald Prison. BJ commits to go and free them from the prison and Anya pledges her help. They must first make it to the prison by passing a number of Nazi chekpoints.

Spoiler: Anya’s grandparents drive them through each checkpoint as BJ kills a large number of Nazi soldiers and opens the main gate to get through the checkpoints. The group makes it through each checkpoint to get on the night train to Berlin.

<

CHAPTER 04

>

Eisenwald Prison

In this chapter, BJ goes to get coffee for Anya and himself while on the night train, when he is stopped by a Nazi officer named Frau Engel. She interrogates BJ to see if he is an Aryan or not. BJ makes it back to their cabin with the coffee and they shortly arrive in Berlin and make their way to Eisenwald Prison. BJ sneaks onto the roof of the prison and slowly makes his way to where the resistance fighters are held.

Spoiler: The resistance fighters are freed and BJ pulls off the mask of one of the prisoners to find it's an old friend. Note: depending on who you saved, it is either Fergus or Wyatt. They make it out of the prison and to the resistance HQ called Kreisau Circle.

<

CHAPTER 05

>

A New Home

In this chapter, you have the opportunity to meet every member of the resistance. As you meet each member of the resistance there is a constant theme. Each member has their own unique lifestyle while some are even disabled. They all share the fact that they would be killed by the Nazi forces if found due to their weaknesses. The goal in this chapter is to get the plans for Project Whisper where the leader of the resistance, Caroline Becker, leads them to a new kind of undetecable aircraft that will give the resistance the opportunity to strike at the Nazi forces from anywhere.

Spoiler: To execute on Project Whisper, the resistance fighter, Bobby Bram drives BJ to the London Nautica and lets BJ out with a headset to communicate with the resistance. Once BJ exits the vehicle, Bobby Bram drives into the building and detonates a suicide bomb blowing an entrace for BJ into the London Nautica.

<

CHAPTER 06

>

London Nautica

In this chapter, you fight through a seemingly endless amount of Nazi soliders. You battle through the wreckage left from Bobby Bram’s suicide bombing, and then on to a Moon auditorium with ending up in a Da'at Yichud laboratory where you find one of the most important weapons in the game, the Laserkraftwerk. Once BJ enters into the hangar where the undetectable aircraft are held, BJ finds it very heavily guarded.

Spoiler: BJ fights off a large amount of Nazi soldiers and then a final Mech boss to open the hatch above to let in the resistance fighters flying in on hang gliders. BJ picks up Caroline and walks her to her aircraft.

<

CHAPTER 07

>

A Mystery

In this chapter, the resistance is tipped of to the fact that the Nazi forces may be using a malfunctioning cement compound. The concrete is growing mold all over the city. BJ is tasked with finding a sample of the concrete in the resistance HQ, but when he goes to pick up the saw to cut the concrete he falls through the floor and must fight his way back from the sewers.

Spoiler: From the artifacts gathered in the London Nautica in the Da'at Yichud laboratory, the resistance put together the fact that a name on the list in the laboratory was also found on a list in a Croatian work camp. The name is Set Roth, the person who is tampering with the cement and making it decay.

<

CHAPTER 08

>

Camp Belica

In this chapter, BJ intentionally gets caught and sent to Camp Belica where they believe Set Roth is being held prisoner. When BJ enters the camp, he is set to make the concrete for the Reich. A fellow prisoner, named Bombate, tells him that he can find Set Roth in a different camp, but he needs to do something before he can make it to that part of the prison.

Spoiler: BJ sabotages the equipement causing the guards to call the maintenance crew. BJ trades clothes with another prisoner and is able to meet with Set Roth. Set Roth agrees to join the resistance, but in order to break out BJ needs to get him a battery. He gets the battery, the mech guard is turned on the Nazi guards and the resistance fighters escape the prison.

<

CHAPTER 09

>

New Tactics

In this chapter, BJ is sent on more errands similar to finding the saw to cut the moldy concrete. BJ jumps into the sewer to get the welder that was dropped in there so Set can build a mini glider in order to accomplish their next mission.

Spoiler: BJ has to fight off the Nazi forces in the sewer and he brings back the welder to build the mini glider so they can capture a Nazi u-boat.

<

CHAPTER 10

>

Berlin Catacombs

In this chapter, BJ is using the built mini glider to go through the sewers below a Nazi train station. There are a few puzzles that BJ has to solve and once out of the sewers, BJ is to capture a train with nuclear missiles made for the u-boat the resistance wants to capture. BJ is tasked with having to kill a large number of Nazi soldiers in the train station.

Spoiler: BJ kills all the Nazi soldiers and steals the train. The resistance takes out the nuclear missile and loads BJ into the missile casing so he can be shipped on to the Nazi u-boat.

<

CHAPTER 11

>

U-Boat

In this chapter, BJ gets out of the missile casing and is tasked with taking over the u-boat. There are a large number of Nazi forces on the u-boat, and BJ must get to the control room to put up the communications buoys.

Spoiler: BJ makes it through all the Nazi soldiers, raises the buoys, and is able to take full control of the u-boat so they can go with Set Roth to the secret Da'at Yichud vault deep under the ocean.

<

CHAPTER 12

>

Gibraltar Bridge

In this chapter, BJ is tasked with getting the launch codes for the u-boat’s nuclear missiles. After they went to the Da'at Yichud vault they brought a new piece of tech that once deployed decimates everything around it with a pulley system. BJ unleashes this new tech on the bridge that is running a train with a Nazi officer who has the Lunar Base credentials to gain access to the nuclear codes.

Spoiler: BJ kills every Nazi soldier that was recovering from the crash of the train. He locates the officer’s train and climbs down to get the Nazi officer credentials and uses them to get on to the Nazi Lunar Base.

<

CHAPTER 13

>

Lunar Base

In this chapter, BJ is infiltrating the Nazi Lunar Base as an officer and makes it past all the checkpoints. Once he is safely inside the Nazi security he changes clothes and kills every Nazi in the Lunar Base and is able to get the nuclear launch codes. BJ now must escape the Lunar Base and return to the resistance so they can use the codes in the u-boat to take out Deathshead.

Spoiler: BJ gets the codes and boards a tram that gets show down from a Nazi mech soldier. He does escape barely by hijacking a spaceship and flies back to Earth where he crash lands into the London Nautica.

<

CHAPTER 14

>

Return to London Nautica

In this chapter, BJ must fight through all the Nazi soldiers in the building that he crashed into. He hears on his radio comms that the resistance base has been found and that they are currently under attack. BJ does his best to fight his way back to the resistance base. When he is almost free, he then runs into the London Monitor.

Spoiler: BJ takes down the London Monitor, the most powerful weapon the Nazi forces have. He fights his way to the resistance base and finds Klaus shot with Max Haas trying to save Klaus.

<

CHAPTER 15

>

Under Attack

In this chapter, BJ makes his way back to the resistance base and does everything he can to save the resistance fighters from Deathshead’s elite force. He talks with many for the last time and fights through all the soldiers to be picked up by Caroline.

Spoiler: Caroline who was paralyzed from battles many years before is now walking and fighting with a suit she found in the Da'at Yichud vault. They successfully escape their base at Kreisau Circle and board the u-boat, their last safe place to plan and execute an attack against Deathshead’s compound.

<

CHAPTER 16

Return to Deathshead’s Compound

In this chapter, they safely make it out of the attack from Deathshead’s elite force and now have everything they need to launch a nuclear attack against Deathshead’s compound. They arrive at the compound and send in BJ to save the resistance fighters that were captured from the previous attack on the resistance base. They use the same tech they used on the Gibraltar bridge and tear the face off the compound allowing BJ to enter unnoticed for a moment.

Spoiler: BJ makes it all the way into the base, and frees the resistance fighters. BJ has a score to settle though, and sets out to kill Deathshead. After a tough fight BJ kills Deathshead, but Deathshead was holding a grenade when BJ comes in to take the final blow. They both blow up and BJ who believes he is a goner tells the resistance to launch a nuke on the compound even though he is still left behind. BJ seemingly dies as the compound is destroyed with a nuclear missile.
