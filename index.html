Chapter 1- Deathshead's Compoud

In this chapter, you are introduced to the main character and his supporting character while flying a bomber assaulting a Nazi compound. You play Captain William "B.J" Blazkowicz as a special forces soldier assaulting Deathshead’s compound. The two supporting characters are Fergus and Wyatt, Fergus is flying the bomber as a clearly experienced vet to war while Wyatt is a young wet-behind-the-ears soldier who is constantly on the verge of losing their mind from the war around them. You immediately see how outnumbered the allied forces are, but Blazko and his team push on and make it to the inner compound. This is where Blazko needs to make a very difficult decision. The story splits into two timelines at this point. You can play the Fergus timeline or the Wyatt timeline.

Spoiler: At the end of the chapter, Deathshead has you choose either Fergus to kill or Wyatt to kill. You find out later they are not dead but extracted from their bodies and put into a war machine.

Chapter 2 - Asylum

This chapter begins fourteen years after the attack on Deathshead’s compound. Blazko was rescued by a family of doctors after taking shrapnel to the back of his head. Over the fourteen years, Blazko is in a vegetative state where he is cared for by Anya. Anya is a new supporting character that Blazko spends a lot of time with throughout the campaign. Blazko comes awake when he is urged to protect the family after a Nazi purge gone wrong leads to the death of the staff, disabled patients and Anya’s family. Blazko must regain his consciousness and save Anya.

Spoiler: Anya is carried to a car where you can rescue her. In the middle of the game, Blazko and Anya fall in love and fight together to the end where Blazko ends up dying while dreaming of spending a normal life with Anya.
